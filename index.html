Our Story

Who We Are & What We Stand For

Founded in 2010, Tarang Hazaribagh has been at the forefront of social change in Jharkhand. We believe every individual deserves dignity, opportunity, and hope.

📚
Quality Education
Free schooling, scholarships and digital literacy for tribal children.
🏥
Healthcare Access
Mobile medical camps and maternal care across remote villages.
🤝
Women Empowerment
Skill training and microfinance for women entrepreneurs.
