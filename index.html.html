Our Story

Who We Are & What We Stand For

Founded in 2010, Tarang Hazaribagh has been at the forefront of social change in Jharkhand. We believe every individual deserves dignity, opportunity, and hope — regardless of where they were born.

📚
Quality Education
Free schooling, scholarships and digital literacy for children from tribal communities.
🏥
Healthcare Access
Mobile medical camps, maternal care and mental health support across remote villages.
🤝
Women Empowerment
Skill training, self-help groups and microfinance for women entrepreneurs.
